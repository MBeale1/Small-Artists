Tobi Lou

Hailing from Chicago Tobi Lou is a rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer that started in 2015. In February 2018, he released the tobi lou and the Moon project which eventually led to the creation of a music video group featuring Smino and a large growth to his platform.
